Welcome to Tzaneen Cycling Club


Tzaneen cycling is a road & mtb cycling club for the Tzaneen community & surrounding areas.

Club Benefits

  • Receiving weekly emails of grouprides
  • Social get-togethers & braais, Year-end function
  • Riding / racing in Tzaneen Cycling club-kit
Joining fee of R800 (2015) Includes club riding shirt & socks. Already a member? - Annual clubfee of R300 pp.

Community

  • In 2014 we have contributed to a R96 000 SANRAL project to safer roads for cyclists by placing Cycling signboards on the roads in and around Tzaneen.
  • We have also built a XCO track and have presented the first ever XCO provincial race in Tzaneen.
  • In 2015 we have plans to take Enduro riding to the next level with Downhillers from all over to come and ride our challenging Enduro track.
  • Our club has also been involved with some fundraisings of charity organizations like Gods Haven, Noah's Ark and Tzaneen Care Group (Cancer).
